Good to Know

Frequently Asked

A few details that will make the weekend smoother. If anything else comes up, we will continue to add it here.

What is the dress code?

<

Black tie. Floor-length gowns, formal cocktail dresses, tuxedos, or dark suits with black tie are all in line with the evening.

When should I arrive?

<

Please plan to arrive 20 to 30 minutes before the ceremony so everyone is settled before we begin promptly at 4:00 PM.

Will transportation be provided?

<

We are not arranging shuttles, so rideshare is the easiest option for the wedding day, especially for the transition from the coast to the reception.

Can I drive and park at the venues?

<

Street parking is available but can be limited near the coast. If you are staying nearby, we strongly recommend rideshare to keep the day simple.
